Admission Process

Regional Institute of Nursing is dedicated to providing quality nursing education. Our admission process is designed to identify candidates who demonstrate the potential to excel in the field of nursing.

Eligibility Criteria
  • Candidates must have passed 10+2 (12th) examination from a recognized board with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology as main subjects.
  • Minimum aggregate marks: 45% for General category, 40% for SC/ST category.
  • Candidates should be between 17-35 years of age as of December 31st of the admission year.
  • Must be medically fit.
Required Documents
  • 10th Mark Sheet and Certificate
  • 12th Mark Sheet and Certificate
  • Transfer Certificate
  • Migration Certificate
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • PRC (Permanent Resident Certificate)
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Medical Fitness Certificate
Online Application Process

To apply for admission to Regional Institute of Nursing, follow these steps:

  1. Register: Create an account in our student portal to begin your application process.
  2. Complete Application: Fill out the online application form with your personal and academic details.
  3. Upload Documents: Scan and upload all required documents.
  4. Review and Submit: Review your application for accuracy before submitting.
  5. Track Status: Log in to your account anytime to track your application status.
